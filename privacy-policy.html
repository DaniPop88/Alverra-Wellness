Privacy Policy

At Alverra Wellness Haven, your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. We are committed to treating your personal information with respect and care, ensuring that you feel secure every step of your wellness journey.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our website, book appointments, or purchase products, we may collect certain information to serve you better. This includes your name, contact details, address, and any information relevant to your wellness preferences.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to provide our services, process orders, improve our offerings, and communicate with you regarding appointments, products, or promotions. Your data is never shared or sold to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to keep your information safe, including encrypted storage and secure access controls. Only authorized staff may view your data as needed to fulfill your requests.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You may request access to your personal data at any time.
  • You may update or correct your information by contacting us directly.
  • You can opt out of promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions provided.
  • If you have concerns about your privacy, contact us at contact@alverrawellness.life.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze usage trends. You can adjust your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to check this page periodically for the latest information.

Thank you for trusting Alverra Wellness Haven. Your privacy is our promise.
